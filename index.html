Emergency Department Assistant

How to use this Assistant

Thank you for using our Assistant, the aim of this is to help students and younger doctors who may be exposed to an emergency setting for the first time.

This Assistant will use various rules and calculations to help guide you while managing your patient and advise on certain actions that can be taken if abnormalities are found.
DISCLAIMER This Assistant is not a substitute for clinical examination and assessment, but rather an Assistant that will quickly bring up values/doses/actions that are common best practice in an ED.
!! The publishers of this site accept no liability for any injury caused to patients, please use your clinical judgement first and foremost, with this guide to be used mainly to improve understanding and formats ,and validate calculations The calculators will assist you in making various observations and help your clinical decision making.

Please enjoy using it and I hope it will help you save lives and improve patient care.
