About this assistant

Thank you for using the Emergency Department Assistant. This tool was created to support medical students and junior doctors working in emergency settings, particularly in resource-constrained environments.

It provides history and examination prompts, calculators, and checklists to help you structure your assessment and management. It is not a substitute for a thorough clinical examination, senior input, or your hospital's protocols.

Use your clinical judgement first, then use this assistant to enhance your understanding, organisation, and productivity on shift.

Values are based on South African and British standards unless otherwise stated.

How to get the most out of it

  • Bookmark this site on your phone home screen.
  • Use the tools and checklists at the bedside while seeing patients.
  • Review sections after shifts to consolidate learning.
