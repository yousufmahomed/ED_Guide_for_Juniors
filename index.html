Emergency Department Assistant

About this Assistant

Thank you for using our Emergency Department Assistant. This tool is designed to help medical students and young doctors in emergency settings. It provides history, clinical examination guides, calculators, and checklists to assist you in patient management.

Please note that this Assistant is not a substitute for clinical examination and assessment. Use your clinical judgment first and foremost, and use this tool to enhance your understanding and productivity.

Enjoy using it to improve patient care. Check back for updates and join our learning group and Twitter feed for more.
