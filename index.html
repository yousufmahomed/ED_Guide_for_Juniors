Emergency Department Assistant

About this Assistant

Thank you for using our Emergency Department Assistant, the aim of it is to help medical students and younger doctors who may be exposed to an emergency setting for the first time.

This Assistant will use guides/rules and calculators to help you while managing your patient and where applicable will provide insight and guidance on findings.
DISCLAIMER This Assistant is not a substitute for clinical examination and assessment, but rather an Assistant that will quickly bring up values/doses/actions that are common best practice in an ED.
!! The developer of this site accepts no liability for any injury or harm caused to patients from use of the site, please use your clinical judgement first and foremost, with this guide to be used mainly to improve understanding and productivity.
The calculators will assist you in making various observations and help your clinical decision making.

Please enjoy using it and I hope it will help you save lives and improve patient care.

Currently the assistant is based on an ADULT patient, but we hope to expand to pediatric and geriatric patients in the near future

Also, please note that the values are based on the South African/British values.

To begin, choose a category below, you have the choice of guides and calculators for a generic consultation.
