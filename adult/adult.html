POCUS & E-FAST for Trauma

POCUS and the E-FAST exam are essential for the assessment of trauma patients. However, it can be challenging to understand the nuances of this topic through a static page alone, as it requires a dynamic understanding of patient and probe positioning, as well as real-time visualization of abnormalities.

To make it easier for you, Dr Johnny Wilkinson has compiled a comprehensive playlist on his YouTube channel . This playlist is dedicated to helping you understand POCUS and the E-FAST exam in detail.

In addition, we also have our own playlist of emergency ultrasound examinations . While these videos are more in-depth, they may not be as well-produced as the above playlist.
