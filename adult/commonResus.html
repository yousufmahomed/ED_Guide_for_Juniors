⚠️ Important Safety Notes

  • Adult doses only: These are typical adult emergency doses. Always use paediatric charts / Broselow tape / local paeds protocols for children.
  • Follow your local guidelines: Always check your hospital / provincial protocols (e.g. ACLS/ATLS/EMGuidance/SAMF) for exact doses, dilutions, and indications.
  • High-risk drugs: Adrenaline, amiodarone, insulin, sodium bicarbonate, calcium, labetalol and magnesium are high-risk – double-check dose, concentration, and route with a senior or colleague before giving.
  • Not a substitute for clinical judgement: This table is a quick reference, not a prescribing guideline. Always consider comorbidities (renal/hepatic failure, pregnancy, elderly, etc.).
  • Monitor continuously: For IV cardioactive drugs (amiodarone, labetalol, magnesium, insulin, etc.) ensure cardiac monitoring, frequent BP checks, and appropriate IV access.
