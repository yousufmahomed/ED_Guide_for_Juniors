Jaundice

Clinical features: Yellowing of the skin and eyes, dark urine, pale stools

Symptoms: Fatigue, weakness, abdominal pain, itching

Causes: Liver disease, hemolytic anemia, bile duct obstruction

Relevant investigations: Liver function tests, bilirubin levels, ultrasound, CT scan

Anaemia

Clinical features: Pallor, fatigue, shortness of breath, tachycardia

Symptoms: Weakness, dizziness, headache, chest pain

Causes: Iron deficiency, vitamin deficiency, chronic disease, blood loss

Relevant investigations: Complete blood count, iron studies, vitamin levels, stool occult blood test

Cyanosis

Clinical features: Blueish discoloration of the skin, lips, and mucous membranes

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, confusion, chest pain

Causes: Hypoxia, heart or lung disease, cold exposure

Relevant investigations: Pulse oximetry, arterial blood gas analysis, chest X-ray, echocardiogram

Clubbing

Clinical features: Enlargement of the fingertips, nail bed softening, curved nails

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, chest pain, coughing up blood

Causes: Lung disease, heart disease, liver disease

Relevant investigations: Chest X-ray, CT scan, pulmonary function tests, echocardiogram

Oedema

Clinical features: Swelling of the feet, ankles, legs, or abdomen

Symptoms: Shortness of breath, fatigue, weight gain

Causes: Heart failure, kidney disease, liver disease, lymphatic obstruction

Relevant investigations: Blood tests, urine tests, chest X-ray, echocardiogram

Lymphadenopathy

Clinical features: Enlarged lymph nodes, often in the neck, armpits, or groin

Symptoms: Fatigue, fever, night sweats, weight loss

Causes: Infection, inflammation, cancer

Relevant investigations: Blood tests, imaging studies (CT scan, MRI), lymph node biopsy

Dehydration

Clinical features: Dry mouth, decreased urine output, thirst, dry skin, sunken eyes, cool extremities, low blood pressure

Symptoms: Fatigue, dizziness, confusion, rapid heartbeat, headache

Causes: Excessive sweating, diarrhea, vomiting, fever, decreased fluid intake, diabetes, certain medications

Relevant investigations: Blood tests (electrolyte levels, kidney function tests), urine tests (specific gravity, urine osmolality), physical examination
