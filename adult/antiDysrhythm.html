Antidysrhythmic Drugs

Quick reference to commonly used antidysrhythmic drugs in the Emergency Department. Always confirm doses and indications with your local hospital protocols and reference texts.

Class Drug Dosage Route Indication
Class I Lidocaine (Xylocaine) 1–1.5 mg/kg bolus, then 0.5–0.75 mg/kg every 5–10 minutes as needed Intravenous Ventricular tachycardia, ventricular fibrillation
Procainamide (Procan, Procanbid) 20–30 mg/min, maximum total dose 17 mg/kg Intravenous Ventricular tachycardia, atrial fibrillation, atrial flutter
Class II Propranolol (Inderal) 1–3 mg IV over 2–3 minutes, may repeat every 2–5 minutes; max 10 mg Intravenous Supraventricular tachycardia, ventricular arrhythmias
Metoprolol (Lopressor) 5 mg IV over 1–2 minutes, repeat every 5 minutes as needed; max 15 mg Intravenous Supraventricular tachycardia, ventricular arrhythmias
Class III Amiodarone (Cordarone, Pacerone) 150–300 mg over 10–20 minutes as a loading dose, then 1 mg/min for 6 hours, followed by 0.5 mg/min for 18 hours (or as per local protocol) Intravenous Ventricular arrhythmias, atrial fibrillation, atrial flutter
Ibutilide (Corvert) 1 mg over 10 minutes; may repeat once with 0.5–1 mg after 10 minutes if needed Intravenous Atrial fibrillation, atrial flutter
Class IV Verapamil (Calan, Isoptin) 5–10 mg IV over 2–3 minutes; may repeat once after 30 minutes if necessary Intravenous Supraventricular tachycardia
Diltiazem (Cardizem) 0.25 mg/kg IV over 2 minutes, then infusion 5–15 mg/hour Intravenous Supraventricular tachycardia, rate control in atrial fibrillation
First Line Adenosine (Adenocard) 6 mg rapid IV bolus followed by 20 mL saline flush; if no response after 1–2 minutes, give 12 mg followed by 20 mL flush (may repeat once as per protocol) Intravenous (rapid bolus) Paroxysmal supraventricular tachycardia (PSVT)

Note: Above doses are typical examples. Always check patient factors (age, weight, comorbidities, renal/hepatic function), concurrent medications and your local Emergency Department / ACLS guidelines before administration.
