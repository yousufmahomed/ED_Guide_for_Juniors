Commonly Used Analgesics in Hospitals

Analgesic Dosage Route of Administration

Aspirin 325-650 mg every 4-6 hours Oral
Diclofenac Sodium 50-100 mg every 8-12 hours Oral or intramuscular
Fentanyl 25-100 mcg every 1-2 hours Intravenous, transdermal, or transmucosal
Ibuprofen 200-400 mg every 4-6 hours Oral
Ketorolac 10-30 mg every 4-6 hours Intravenous or intramuscular
Morphine 2.5-10 mg every 4 hours Intravenous, intramuscular, or subcutaneous
Pethidine (Meperidine) 50-150 mg every 4-6 hours (ideally dont go above 75mg without seniors permission!!) Intramuscular or intravenous
Tramadol 50-100 mg every 4-6 hours IOral or intravenous
Oxycodone 5-15 mg every 4-6 hours Oral or intravenous
