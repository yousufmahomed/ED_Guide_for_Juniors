Interpreting CSF Results

Key CSF Parameters and their Normal Values

Parameter Normal Value
Opening Pressure 70-180 mm H2O
Closing Pressure Similar to opening pressure
Appearance Clear and colorless
Total Protein 15-45 mg/dL
Glucose 50-80 mg/dL (or 2/3 of blood glucose)
White Blood Cell (WBC) Count 0-5 cells/µL
Red Blood Cell (RBC) Count 0 cells/µL
