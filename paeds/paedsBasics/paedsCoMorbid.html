Pediatric Past History and Co-Morbid Conditions

Pediatric Co-Morbidities

  • Asthma

    • Ask about triggers, age of onset, medications, and previous hospitalizations.

  • Congenital Heart Defects

    • Ask about surgeries, symptoms, and regular care such as medications or follow-up visits with specialists.

  • Diabetes (Type I mainly)

    • Ask about age of onset, medications, diet, and any history of diabetic ketoacidosis (DKA).

  • Epilepsy

    • Ask about types of seizures, medications, recent changes in behavior or academics, and any developmental delays.

  • Developmental Disorders

    • Include Autism Spectrum Disorders, ADHD, learning disabilities, etc.

  • Growth and Nutritional Concerns

    • Ask about feeding habits, known food allergies, and growth patterns. Important to identify malnutrition or obesity.

  • Genetic Conditions

    • Include information about any known genetic conditions like Down Syndrome, Cystic Fibrosis, etc.

  • Previous Surgeries or Hospitalizations

    • Include surgeries for congenital anomalies or injuries, any hospitalizations for infections or other serious conditions.

  • Immunization Status

    • Important to have an updated immunization record as per the local guidelines.
