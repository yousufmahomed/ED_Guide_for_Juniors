Commonly Used Resuscitation Drugs for Pediatrics

Drug Dosage Route of Administration Indication for Use
Adrenaline (Epinephrine) 0.01 mg/kg/dose (0.1 mL/kg/dose of 1:10,000 solution), every 3-5 minutes Intravenous, intraosseous, or endotracheal Cardiac arrest, anaphylaxis, bronchospasm, hypotension
Amiodarone 5 mg/kg bolus, then 5-10 mg/kg over 20-60 minutes Intravenous Ventricular fibrillation, ventricular tachycardia
Atropine 0.02 mg/kg/dose (minimum dose: 0.1 mg; maximum dose: 0.5 mg) Intravenous, intraosseous, or endotracheal Bradycardia, organophosphate poisoning
Bicarbonate 1-2 mEq/kg/dose (maximum dose: 50 mEq/dose) Intravenous Metabolic acidosis, hyperkalemia
Lidocaine 1 mg/kg/dose, may repeat at 0.5-0.75 mg/kg/dose every 5-10 minutes (maximum dose: 3 mg/kg) Intravenous, intraosseous, or endotracheal Ventricular fibrillation, ventricular tachycardia
Calcium gluconate 100 mg/kg/dose (maximum dose: 1 g/dose) Intravenous Hyperkalemia, hypocalcemia, calcium channel blocker overdose
Furosemide 1 mg/kg/dose intravenous or intramuscular, may repeat every 6 hours as needed Intravenous or intramuscular Acute pulmonary edema, edema associated with heart failure, hypertension
Hydrocortisone 1-2 mg/kg/dose intravenous or intramuscular, every 6 hours as needed Intravenous or intramuscular Adrenal insufficiency, sepsis, anaphylaxis, asthma
Short-acting insulin 0.1-0.2 units/kg/hour intravenous infusion, adjust dose based on blood glucose levels Intravenous Diabetic ketoacidosis, hyperosmolar hyperglycemic state, hyperkalemia
Ipratropium 250-500 mcg (1-2 vials) mixed with 2.5-3 mL of normal saline, administered every 20 minutes up to 3 doses Inhalation Acute exacerbation of asthma or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)
Magnesium sulfate 25-50 mg/kg/dose intravenous over 10-20 minutes (maximum dose: 2 g/dose) Intravenous Torsades de pointes, eclampsia, hypomagnesemia
Promethazine 0.25-1 mg/kg/dose intravenous or intramuscular (maximum dose: 25 mg/dose) Intravenous or intramuscular
Dexamethasone 0.15 mg/kg/dose (maximum dose: 16 mg) Intravenous, intramuscular, or oral Asthma exacerbation, croup, anaphylaxis, acute spinal cord injury
Epinephrine (Adrenaline) 0.01 mg/kg/dose (maximum dose: 0.3 mg/dose) intramuscular Intramuscular Anaphylaxis, severe asthma exacerbation
Ibuprofen 10 mg/kg/dose (maximum dose: 400 mg/dose) Oral Fever, pain, inflammatory conditions
Paracetamol (Acetaminophen) 15 mg/kg/dose (maximum dose: 500 mg/dose) Oral Fever, pain
Salbutamol (Albuterol) 0.15 mg/kg/dose (maximum dose: 5 mg/dose) nebulized or 0.1 mg/kg/dose (maximum dose: 10 mg/dose) via metered-dose inhaler Inhalation Asthma exacerbation, acute bronchiolitis
Ceftriaxone 50-100 mg/kg/dose intravenous or intramuscular (maximum dose: 2 g/dose) Intravenous or intramuscular Bacterial infections
Ampicillin 50-100 mg/kg/dose intravenous or intramuscular (maximum dose: 2 g/dose) Intravenous or intramuscular Bacterial infections
Gentamicin 4-7.5 mg/kg/dose intravenous or intramuscular (maximum dose: 240 mg/dose) Intravenous or intramuscular Bacterial infections
Phenobarbital 15-20 mg/kg/dose intravenous (maximum rate: 100 mg/minute) Intravenous Seizures
Phenytoin 15-20 mg/kg/dose intravenous (maximum rate: 50 mg/minute) Intravenous Seizures
